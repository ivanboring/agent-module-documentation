Deprecated Metatag submodule for the defunct Google+ social network; do not use — it will be removed in Metatag 3.0.0.

---

Google+ was shut down by Google in 2019, so the meta tags this submodule once provided are obsolete. It is marked `hidden` and `deprecated` in the codebase and remains only for backward compatibility with old sites. Do not enable it on new sites; remove it where present. Depends on the main Metatag module.
